Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sathi

Creating an account on Sathi is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar, fill in your details including your name, email, username, and password. Once you submit the form, you'll be redirected to the login page where you can sign in with your new credentials.
If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. Follow the link in the email to create a new password for your account.
Yes! You can customize your profile by going to Settings. There you can update your profile picture, bio, location, and other personal information. Make your profile unique and let others know more about you!
To follow someone, visit their profile page and click the "Follow" button. Once you follow someone, their posts will appear in your home feed. You can unfollow them at any time by clicking the "Unfollow" button on their profile.
We take content moderation seriously. If you see inappropriate content, please contact our support team through the contact page with details about the content. Our moderation team will review and take appropriate action to maintain a safe environment for all users.
Yes! We prioritize user privacy and data security. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties without your consent. You have full control over your privacy settings and can choose who sees your content.
Yes, you can delete your account at any time from your account settings. Please note that deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your posts, photos, and data will be permanently removed from our servers.
To upload content, click the "Upload" button in the navigation bar. You can then select an image from your device, add a caption, and post it to your feed. Your followers will be able to see, like, and comment on your posts.
